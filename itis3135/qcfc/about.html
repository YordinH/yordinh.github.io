About Queen City Fitness Coaching

Queen City Fitness Coaching was created to help people in Charlotte and beyond gain confidence in the gym, build strong habits, and achieve real results through smart, simple, personalized training programs.

Our coaching philosophy is based on meeting people where they are. Whether you're brand new to fitness or returning after time away, we break things down into easy-to-follow steps while building strength, endurance, and consistency.
