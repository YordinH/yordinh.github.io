Coaching Services

Queen City Fitness Coaching provides training services designed to help beginners and intermediate lifters build strength, confidence, and consistency. Every service includes personalized guidance and clear step-by-step progressions.

1. One-on-One Personal Training

Individual training sessions focused on your goals, experience level, and schedule. Ideal for beginners or anyone wanting direct technique feedback and structured support.

  • In-person or virtual options
  • Session-by-session feedback
  • Customized weekly training plan

2. Beginner Foundations Program

A 4-week program for people new to lifting. Learn form, build confidence, and create sustainable habits that make the gym less overwhelming.

  • Teaches the essentials (squat, hinge, push, pull)
  • Includes warm-ups, cooldowns, and habit guides
  • Easy-to-follow structure

3. Custom Workout Programming

Receive a tailored 4-week workout plan based on your goals, experience, and schedule. Great for people who want structure without live sessions.

  • Delivered via email or PDF
  • Technique videos included
  • Weekly adjustments if needed
