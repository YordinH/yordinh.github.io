Hamster's Yearning

Yearning Hamster is a whimsical subscription company that delivers monthly gourmet snack boxes right to your door. Each box is carefully curated to satisfy those little cravings we all have, from rich chocolates and artisan cookies to unique international treats you won’t find at the corner store. Just like a hamster with cheeks full of goodies, our customers get to indulge in a playful variety of snacks that bring comfort and delight in every bite.

Our mission is simple: make life a little sweeter and a lot more fun. Whether you’re looking to discover new flavors, enjoy a cozy movie night, or just treat yourself after a long day, Yearning Hamster has something to keep your cravings satisfied. Each month is a surprise, offering both seasonal favorites and bold new snacks that keep you coming back for more.
